- Ensure UTF-8 encoding
- Remove empty rows
- Verify at least 2 columns

**Fix:**
- Reload page (voices load on startup)

- Check browser settings â†’ Accessibility â†’ Text-to-Speech

- App remembers your selection
- Voices reload automatically

### "Can't login"
